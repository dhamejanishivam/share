    How to Share Photos/Videos/Files etc

    Ensure both devices are on the same WiFi or Hotspot.

    1. Open the Website on both your devices.
    2. On Device A, click HOST. It will generate a QR code and a Code.
    3. On Device B, Scan the QR Code with your camera or Enter the 6-Digit code. It will auto-join
    4. Wait 1-2 seconds. The status will turn Green (Connected).
    5. Select files or drag and drop to transfer instantly.



    How to use Share Text

    Ensure both devices have internet connection, they don't need to be on the same network.

    1. Open the Website on both your devices.
    2. Click "Share Text" on navigation bar at center top.
    3. On Device A, enter the text that you want to share in the textbox. (or you can also paste it directly with using the 'paste' button).
    4. Click 'Share Text' button highlighted in Blue color. You will get a 6-Digit code
    5. Share that 6-Digit code with Person B
    6. On Device B, click the input tab next to 'Receive' and enter that 6-Digit code and press 'Get' button
    7. The shared text from Person 1 will be automatically visible on your screen, you can copy it directly by clicking the 'Copy' button.

    Tip : The Expiration time on the 'Share Text' page is the amount of time after which your message will be auto deleted from the server.

    About InstaDrop

    Fast, direct, and completely private.

    What is InstaDrop?

    InstaDrop is a simple web app to send files and text between your devices. Instead of uploading your data to a cloud, it connects your devices directly to each other. This makes transfers fast and highly secure.

    Privacy & Safety

    Your data belongs to you. Here is exactly how we keep it safe:

    • Files are never stored: When you send a file, it travels directly from one device to the other. It never touches our servers. We cannot see, copy, or keep your files.
    • Text auto-deletes: Any text you share is temporarily held so the other device can receive it. By default, it is completely deleted from our database after 1 hour. You can extend this, but it is always erased forever when the time runs out.
    • No tracking: We do not monitor your connections, read your shared text, or keep logs of what you do.
