    How to Connect

    Ensure both devices are on the same WiFi or Hotspot.

    1. Open the app on both your PC and your Mobile device.
    2. On Device A, click HOST. It will generate a QR code.
    3. On Device B, Scan the QR Code with your camera. It will auto-join!
    4. Wait 1-2 seconds. The status will turn Green (Connected).
    5. Select files or drag and drop to transfer instantly.
